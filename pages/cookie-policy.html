Cookie Policy

How we use cookies to improve your experience.

📅 Last Updated: January 15, 2026

What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files that are stored on your device when you visit a website. They help us remember your preferences, understand how you use our platform, and improve your experience.

Cookie Tip: You can control cookie settings in your browser preferences at any time.

Types of Cookies We Use

We use the following types of cookies on EduTrack:

Type Purpose Duration Category
Session Cookies Keep you logged in during your session Session Essential
Preference Cookies Remember your theme, language, and settings 1 year Functional
Analytics Cookies Understand how users interact with our platform 2 years Analytics
Security Cookies Protect against fraud and unauthorized access Session Essential
Marketing Cookies Personalize content and offers (optional) 1 year Marketing

How We Use Cookies

1. Essential Cookies

  • Enable core functionality like authentication
  • Maintain secure sessions
  • Remember your login status
  • Protect against security threats

2. Functional Cookies

  • Remember your theme preference (dark/light mode)
  • Save your language settings
  • Store your dashboard layout preferences
  • Remember your notification settings

3. Analytics Cookies

  • Track page visits and time spent
  • Understand which features are most used
  • Identify areas for improvement
  • Measure platform performance

Your Cookie Choices

You have full control over cookies:

  • Accept All: Allow all cookies for the best experience
  • Essential Only: Only allow necessary cookies
  • Custom Settings: Choose which cookies to enable
  • Browser Settings: Manage cookies through your browser

🔧Browser Controls: Most browsers allow you to control cookies through their settings. You can delete existing cookies and prevent future cookies from being stored.

Third-Party Cookies

We may use third-party services that place cookies on your device:

  • Google Analytics: For platform analytics
  • Chart.js: For interactive charts
  • Font Awesome: For icons
  • Google Fonts: For typography

Updates to This Policy

We may update this Cookie Policy to reflect changes in technology or regulations. We'll notify you of significant changes via email or platform notification.
