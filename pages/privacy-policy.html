Privacy Policy

Your privacy matters to us. Here's how we handle your data.

📅 Last Updated: January 15, 2026

Introduction

At EduTrack, we take your privacy seriously. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our school management platform.

Please read this privacy policy carefully. By using EduTrack, you consent to the data practices described in this policy.

Key Principle: We only collect data that helps us provide better educational management services. We never sell your data to third parties.

Information We Collect

1. Personal Information

  • Student Data: Names, grades, attendance records, academic performance
  • Teacher Data: Names, contact information, assigned subjects
  • Parent/Guardian Data: Contact information, relationships to students
  • Admin Data: Login credentials, role permissions, activity logs

2. Usage Data

  • Browser type and version
  • Device information
  • Pages visited and time spent
  • Features used and interactions
  • IP addresses (anonymized)

3. Cookies & Tracking

  • Session cookies for authentication
  • Preference cookies for theme and language settings
  • Analytics cookies for performance improvement

How We Use Your Information

  • School Management: To manage students, subjects, results, and reports
  • Communication: To send notifications, reports, and important updates
  • Improvement: To analyze usage and improve our platform
  • Security: To protect against unauthorized access
  • Compliance: To meet legal and regulatory requirements

Data Sharing & Disclosure

We only share your data in the following circumstances:

  • With your consent: When you give us explicit permission
  • Service Providers: Trusted third parties who assist with hosting, email, and analytics
  • Legal Requirements: When required by law or to protect rights
  • School Administration: Between authorized school staff members

Data Security: We implement robust security measures including encryption, access controls, and regular security audits to protect your data.

Data Retention

We retain your data for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide services. You may request deletion of your data at any time.

  • Student Records: Retained for 5 years after graduation
  • Account Data: Retained until account deletion request
  • Analytics Data: Anonymized and retained for 2 years

Your Rights

You have the following rights regarding your data:

  • Access: Request a copy of your data
  • Correction: Update inaccurate information
  • Deletion: Request data removal (subject to legal requirements)
  • Restriction: Limit how we use your data
  • Portability: Receive your data in a structured format
  • Opt-out: Unsubscribe from marketing communications

Children's Privacy

EduTrack is designed for use by schools and educational institutions. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13 without parental consent. Schools are responsible for obtaining appropriate consent from parents/guardians.

International Data Transfers

Your data may be processed in countries where we or our service providers operate. We ensure appropriate safeguards are in place for international transfers.

Updates to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any material changes by posting the new policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date.
